Extraterrestrial Solar System

Andrew Bunn & Joel Tanig

Features:
- Multiple dynamic lights in the form of the sun and a moon that casts light on its planet.
- A freeroam camera that explores the space.
- Unique textures applied to various spheres.
- Parent child relationships in the form of planets orbiting a sun as well as a light that is a child of a
    moon orbiting a planet that orbits the sun.

Challenge Feature:
- Sky dome: implemented a sky dome that moves with the camera, but does not pan or rotate with it.
